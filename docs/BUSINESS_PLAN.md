# Business Plan: EchoScribe AI

## 1. Executive Summary
EchoScribe AI is a high-performance web application designed to solve the "audio note fatigue" problem. By leveraging state-of-the-art AI transcription and large language models (LLMs), EchoScribe converts raw audio recordings from any language into structured, actionable text summaries.

## 2. Problem Statement
*   **Time Consumption:** Listening to hours of recordings is inefficient.
*   **Accessibility:** Audio notes are hard to search and index.
*   **Language Barriers:** Transcribing non-native languages manually is difficult for many users.
*   **Lack of Structure:** Raw transcriptions are often long and hard to digest without summarization.

## 3. Solution
EchoScribe provides a seamless workflow:
1.  **Upload:** Support for major audio formats (MP3, WAV, M4A, AAC).
2.  **Transcribe:** High-fidelity transcription using OpenAI Whisper or similar high-accuracy models.
3.  **Summarize:** Context-aware summarization that identifies key points, action items, and sentiment.
4.  **Organize:** A clean dashboard to manage and search through past transcriptions.

## 4. Market Analysis
### Target Market
*   **Students & Academics:** For lecture notes and research interviews.
*   **Corporate Professionals:** For meeting minutes and brainstorming sessions.
*   **Content Creators:** For podcast scripts and video subtitles.
*   **Journalists:** For transcribing interviews quickly.

### Competitive Advantage
*   **Simplicity:** Zero-friction UX.
*   **Quality:** Higher accuracy through advanced AI models.
*   **Aesthetics:** Premium design that feels like a professional tool, not just a utility.

## 5. Revenue Model (Future)
*   **Free Tier:** Up to 10 minutes of audio per month.
*   **Pro Tier ($15/mo):** Unlimited transcriptions, priority processing, advanced summary styles.
*   **Enterprise:** API access and team collaboration features.

## 6. Roadmap
*   **Phase 1 (MVP):** Basic upload, transcription, and summarization.
*   **Phase 2:** User authentication, history, and search.
*   **Phase 3:** Real-time transcription and direct recording from browser.
*   **Phase 4:** Integration with Notion, Slack, and Google Drive.
